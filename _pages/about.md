About me
======
I am a postdoctoral fellow at The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center. My research focuses on applying computational approaches to single-cell genomics and transcriptomics to understand the earliest stages of cancer development. I am currently studying the genotypic and phenotypic variation underlying pre-malignant clonal expansions in breast tissue, with the goal of building scalable methods for early cancer detection. I completed my PhD at the Tri-Institutional PhD Program in Computational Biology and Medicine. My doctoral work integrated multi-modal single-cell datasets to characterize the landscape of mitochondrial DNA heterogeneity and clonal dynamics in human cancers, resulting in two first-author publications in Nature Genetics.
